# Safety Protocol & Procedures

## Overview
This document outlines essential safety guidelines and emergency procedures for all Walmart associates.

## Key Safety Areas

### 1. Emergency Procedures
- Know the location of emergency exits
- Familiarize yourself with emergency alarms and procedures
- In case of emergency, follow instructions from management

### 2. Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
- Always wear required PPE when working in designated areas
- Inspect PPE before use
- Replace damaged or worn equipment immediately

### 3. Incident Reporting
- Report all incidents, no matter how minor
- Document incidents thoroughly
- Follow up with management

### 4. Hazard Communication
- Understand SDS (Safety Data Sheets)
- Know the hazards of materials you work with
- Ask questions if you're unsure

## Contact
For safety concerns, contact your Store Manager or Safety Coordinator.
