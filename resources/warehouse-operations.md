# Warehouse Operations Guide

Complete guide to warehouse operations, inventory management, and best practices.

## Core Responsibilities
1. Inventory Management - Track and organize merchandise
2. Receiving - Accept and verify incoming shipments
3. Stocking - Place items in correct locations
4. Shipping - Prepare orders for delivery
5. Safety - Maintain safe working environment

## Equipment Operation
- Pallet jacks and forklifts require certification
- Always perform equipment checks before use
- Report malfunctions immediately

Contact your Operations Manager for more information.
