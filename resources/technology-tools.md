# Technology Tools & Systems Guide

Guide to Walmart technology tools and applications.

## Essential Systems

### SMART System
- Main point-of-sale register system
- Used for transactions and inventory tracking
- Access: In-store only

### Walmart One
- Employee portal for benefits
- View pay stubs and schedule
- Request time off
- Access: Online 24/7

### Kronos
- Schedule management
- Time tracking and shift swaps
- Access: In-store kiosks or online

### eScreening
- Performance reviews and goal tracking
- Development plans
- Access: Online through Walmart One

### The Wire
- Internal communications
- Company news and updates
- Store announcements
- Access: In-store computers

## Mobile Apps
- Walmart Me - Mobile employee portal
- WalmartOne+ Store - Training and learning
