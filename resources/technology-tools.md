# Technology Tools & Systems Guide

## Essential Walmart Systems

### SMART System
- Main point-of-sale register system
- Used for transactions
- Tracks inventory
- **Access**: In-store only with login

### Walmart One
- Employee portal for benefits
- View pay stubs
- Request time off
- Update personal information
- **Access**: Online 24/7

### Kronos
- Schedule management
- Time tracking
- Request shift swaps
- View hours
- **Access**: In-store kiosks or online

### eScreening
- Performance reviews
- Goal tracking
- Development plans
- **Access**: Online through Walmart One

### The Wire
- Internal communications
- Company news and updates
- Store announcements
- Policy information
- **Access**: In-store computers

## Mobile Apps

### Walmart Me
- Mobile portal
- Check schedule
- View pay
- Contact HR
- Browse benefits

### WalmartOne+ Store
- Access training materials
- Complete online courses
- View certifications
- Check your profile

## Getting Help
- IT Help Desk: ext. 5000
- Password resets: Walmart One
- System issues: Contact manager
- Training: Ask your lead

## Security Reminders
- Never share passwords
- Lock computer when away
- Report suspicious activity
- Keep data confidential
- Clear cache regularly
