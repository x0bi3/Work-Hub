# Break & Meal Policies

Associate breaks, meal periods, and rest day guidelines.

## Break Entitlements
- Two 15-minute paid breaks per shift (4+ hours)
- One 30-minute unpaid meal break (6+ hours)
- One 60-minute unpaid meal break (8+ hours)

## Guidelines
- Notify supervisor before taking break
- Breaks cannot be combined or waived
- Schedule meal breaks during low-traffic periods
- Coordinate with team members

## Rest Days
- You're entitled to at least one day off per week
- Schedule requests are honored when possible

Contact HR for details specific to your location.
