# Break & Meal Policies

## Break Entitlements

### Paid Breaks
- Two 15-minute paid breaks per shift (shifts 4+ hours)
- Breaks should be spread throughout shift
- Cannot combine breaks

### Meal Periods
- One 30-minute unpaid meal break (shifts 6+ hours)
- One 60-minute unpaid meal break (shifts 8+ hours)
- Schedule meal break based on shift length

## Break Guidelines

### Taking Your Break
- Notify supervisor before taking break
- Leave work area in safe condition
- Clock out appropriately
- Return on time

### Scheduling
- Meal breaks cannot be waived
- Schedule breaks during low-traffic periods
- Coordinate with team members
- Get supervisor approval

## Rest Days
- You're entitled to at least one day off per week
- Schedule requests are honored when possible
- Communicate early about day-off needs

## Questions?
Contact HR or your Manager for details specific to your state/location.
