# Customer Service Standards

Walmart's commitment to customer service excellence and best practices.

## Core Principles
1. Greeting - Welcome customers within 30 seconds
2. Assistance - Actively listen and provide accurate information
3. Respect - Treat all customers with dignity
4. Resolution - Take ownership and solve problems

## The Walmart Way
"Save Money. Live Better." - This guides everything we do.
