# Customer Service Standards

## Walmart's Commitment
Every day, we strive to serve our customers with excellence, respect, and integrity.

## Core Service Principles

### 1. Greeting
- Greet every customer within 30 seconds
- Make eye contact and smile
- Use a friendly tone

### 2. Assistance
- Actively listen to customer needs
- Provide accurate information
- Go the extra mile
- Offer solutions, not excuses

### 3. Respect
- Treat all customers with dignity
- Never discriminate
- Respect personal space
- Be culturally sensitive

### 4. Resolution
- Listen to complaints fully
- Take ownership of issues
- Work toward solutions
- Follow up when necessary

## The Walmart Way
Save money. Live better.
This means helping customers get what they need at the best prices.
