# Loss Prevention Handbook

## Understanding Loss Prevention
Loss prevention protects Walmart and our associates through security procedures and best practices.

## Types of Loss

### Theft
- Internal theft (employee or associate)
- External theft (shoplifting)
- Organized retail crime

### Shrinkage
- Damaged merchandise
- Inventory errors
- Administrative mistakes

### Fraud
- Receipt fraud
- Return fraud
- Register manipulation

## Your Responsibilities

### Vigilance
- Be aware of suspicious activity
- Watch for unusual behavior
- Monitor cash handling
- Verify authenticity of documents

### Reporting
- Report suspicious activity to management
- Document observations carefully
- Never accuse without evidence
- Use proper reporting channels

### Prevention
- Follow cash handling procedures
- Lock up valuables
- Verify customer transactions
- Keep merchandise secure
- Monitor high-risk items

### High-Risk Items
- Electronics
- Cosmetics
- Grocery items
- Office supplies
- Tools

## Security Protocols
- Follow entrance/exit procedures
- Comply with bag checks
- Wear identification at all times
- Keep passwords confidential
- Report system access issues
