# Loss Prevention Handbook

Security procedures and loss prevention best practices for all associates.

## Types of Loss
1. Theft - Internal and external
2. Shrinkage - Damaged merchandise or inventory errors
3. Fraud - Receipt fraud and register manipulation

## Your Responsibilities
- Be aware of suspicious activity
- Report concerns to management
- Follow cash handling procedures
- Keep merchandise secure
- Monitor high-risk items (electronics, cosmetics, tools)

## Prevention Measures
- Lock up valuables
- Verify customer transactions
- Wear identification at all times
- Keep passwords confidential
- Report system access issues

## High-Risk Items
- Electronics
- Cosmetics
- Grocery items
- Office supplies
- Tools

Contact Security Team for loss prevention training.
