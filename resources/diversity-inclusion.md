# Diversity & Inclusion at Walmart

## Our Commitment
Walmart believes that diversity makes us stronger. We're committed to creating an inclusive workplace where every associate feels valued and respected.

## What is Diversity?
- Different backgrounds and experiences
- Various perspectives and viewpoints
- Unique talents and abilities
- Representation across all levels

## What is Inclusion?
- Belonging and acceptance
- Equal opportunity for all
- Voice and participation
- Psychological safety

## Walmart's Diversity Goals
- Increase representation of underrepresented groups
- Promote diverse talent to leadership
- Partner with diverse suppliers
- Support diverse communities
- Foster inclusive culture

## Your Role
- Treat all colleagues with respect
- Listen to different perspectives
- Challenge unconscious bias
- Report discrimination
- Support colleagues from all backgrounds

## Resources
- Employee Resource Groups (ERGs)
- Diversity training programs
- Mentorship opportunities
- Community outreach programs

## Zero Tolerance
Walmart has zero tolerance for discrimination, harassment, or exclusion based on:
- Race or color
- Religion
- Gender
- National origin
- Age
- Disability
- Sexual orientation
- Gender identity
- Veteran status
